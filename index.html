Proposed Solution

Introducing AERIS

AERIS (AI Emotional Risk Prediction & Intervention System) is a prototype AI-driven framework developed to assist institutions in identifying emotional distress patterns through structured mood logging, pattern analysis, and risk scoring mechanisms.

This system is designed for academic environments and integrates contextual factors such as attendance trends and examination cycles.

📝

Structured Monitoring

Daily mood logging through text, emoji, or voice input — enabling consistent emotional data collection with minimal friction.

🔍

Pattern Analysis

NLP and temporal modeling identify emotional trajectory shifts that may indicate emerging distress patterns over time.

📋

Structured Escalation

A 4-level escalation framework routes observations to the appropriate institutional stakeholder based on assessed risk level.

System Workflow

End-to-End Processing Pipeline

A structured 6-step pipeline from student input to institutional review.

1
Student Input

Student logs daily emotion through text, emoji, or voice input.

2
NLP Classification

NLP engine classifies emotional category from input data.

3
Temporal Modeling

LSTM models emotional patterns over time to detect trajectory shifts.

4
Risk Scoring

Risk engine calculates structured score on a 0–100 scale.

5
Escalation Logic

Escalation logic triggers alerts across 4 levels (L1–L4).

6
Dashboard Review

Dashboard updates for institutional review and action tracking.

Core Architecture

Technical Components

Four integrated layers forming the AERIS prediction pipeline.

🤖

NLP Layer

BERT / DistilBERT models classify emotional states from text and voice input.

🧬

Temporal Modeling

LSTM networks detect emotional trajectory shifts across time sequences.

📈

Risk Prediction

XGBoost classifier outputs structured risk score on a 0–100 scale.

📅

Context Fusion

Academic context (attendance, GPA, exams) integrated into prediction pipeline.
