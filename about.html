About AERIS

Developed by Team RTX from Trident Academy of Technology as part of Trithon 2026 Hackathon.

The Project

An AI-Based Approach to Student Wellbeing

AERIS (AI Emotional Risk Prediction & Intervention System) is a prototype framework designed to assist educational institutions in identifying emotional distress patterns among students.

The system integrates NLP-based emotion classification, LSTM temporal modeling, and XGBoost risk scoring with academic contextual data to generate structured risk assessments.

AERIS was developed as part of Trithon 2026, a 24-hour non-stop hackathon organized by Trident Academy of Technology under the theme of innovative AI/ML solutions for education and healthcare.

🎯

Vision Statement

"To build a structured AI-based emotional safety framework for educational institutions."

Hackathon Context

Trithon 2026

Trithon 2026 is a 24-hour non-stop hackathon organized by Trident Academy of Technology, Bhubaneswar.

The hackathon theme focuses on "Innovative Improvement in Agriculture, Healthcare, Environment, Energy, and Education using AI/ML/DL/LLM Technologies."
