AI & Technical Architecture

An overview of the machine learning models and infrastructure components used in the AERIS prototype.

Natural Language Processing

BERT / DistilBERT

Fine-tuned for academic stress classification from student text and voice inputs.

→Contextual emotional classification beyond simple sentiment
→Pre-trained on general corpora, fine-tuned for educational context
→Multi-class output: anxiety, sadness, stress, isolation, neutral, positive
→DistilBERT variant for resource-constrained deployment

Temporal Modeling

LSTM Networks

Time-series modeling of emotional patterns to detect trajectory shifts invisible to single-point analysis.

→Sequence modeling of mood log patterns over weeks
→Detects gradual emotional decline that snapshots miss
→Memory gates retain long-term behavioral context

Risk Classification

XGBoost Classifier

Structured risk scoring algorithm combining multiple feature dimensions into a single risk probability output (0–100).
