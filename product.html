System Design & Functional Architecture

A structured overview of the scoring frameworks, escalation models, and contextual awareness layers within AERIS.

Core Metric

A composite metric scored 0–100, derived from multiple input signals to provide a holistic view of student emotional state.

Risk Assessment

Risk Score Framework

Structured score indicating probability bands used to support counselor prioritization.

0–25
Low

Normal emotional range. Standard monitoring continues.

26–50
Moderate

Patterns emerging. Increased check-ins recommended.

51–75
High

Counselor notification is triggered for review.

76–100
Critical

Immediate institutional review and intervention required.

Escalation Framework

4-Level Escalation Model

A structured response framework that routes observations to appropriate institutional stakeholders based on assessed risk level.

L1
AI Coping Suggestion
System generates contextual coping recommendations for the student.
L2
Teacher Notification
Assigned teacher receives a structured summary with suggested follow-up actions.
L3
Counselor Alert
School counselor is notified with risk breakdown and recommended intervention approach.
L4
Guardian Notification
Institution-controlled guardian notification protocol is initiated for critical-level cases.
